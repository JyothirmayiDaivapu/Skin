1.Oiling


Pamper your scalp with an intense scalp massage using natural hair oils. You will be surprised to know the benefits of oiling your hair. Massage your scalp for about 5-10 minutes, before you apply the oil to your strands.


2.Cleanse


Gently rinse your hair with water before you choose from a wide variety of mild hair shampoos to apply. Massage your scalp for a few minutes to dislodge all the dirt and grime. Finally, wash your hair with plenty of water. This will cleanse your hair thoroughly.


3.Conditioner


Choose from a range of hair conditioners available and select one that suits you best. Apply hair conditioner to your wet strands and wait for a few minutes before rinsing your hair with lukewarm water.
